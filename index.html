Pokémon Arena is a head-to-head battle game inspired by games like Dice Throne.
Players select a Pokémon and use attacks, defenses, and strategy to reduce their opponent’s HP to 0.
The game supports multiplayer and team formats, but this rulebook focuses on 1v1 battles.

Each player:

  • Chooses 1 Pokémon
  • Uses a unique deck
  • Builds their stats over time
  • Uses attacks and defenses to outplay their opponent

Each player selects one Pokemon to play as:

  • Charizard (Fire/Flying)
  • Pikachu (Electric)
  • Exeggutor (Grass/Psychic)
  • Greninja (Water/Dark)
  • Alolan Ninetales (Fairy/Ice)
  • Gengar (Ghost/Poison)
  • Lucario (Fighting/Steel)
  • Garchomp (Ground/Dragon)

Each Pokemon has a character mat, which includes:

  • Passive ability
  • Description
  • Type
  • Innate attack
  • Slots for:
    • 4 attack moves
    • 2 defense moves
